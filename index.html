What We Do

Complete Pool & Garden Services

From sparkling pools to lush lawns — we keep your outdoor spaces looking their absolute best, all year round.

🏊

Pool Cleaning & Maintenance

Regular professional pool cleaning to keep your water crystal clear, balanced, and safe for swimming.

  • Water testing & chemical balancing
  • Vacuuming & surface skimming
  • Filter cleaning & backwashing
  • Pump & equipment inspection
🔧

Pool Repairs & Equipment

Fast, reliable repairs for pumps, filters, chlorinators, and all pool equipment to keep your pool running perfectly.

  • Pump & motor repairs
  • Chlorinator servicing
  • Leak detection & repair
  • Equipment upgrades
🌿

Garden Maintenance

Comprehensive garden care to keep your outdoor space healthy, tidy, and thriving through every season.

  • Lawn mowing & edging
  • Hedge trimming & shaping
  • Weeding & mulching
  • Seasonal planting
✂️

Lawn Care & Landscaping

From regular lawn maintenance to full garden transformations — we bring your outdoor vision to life.

  • Lawn fertilising & aeration
  • Garden bed design
  • Irrigation system setup
  • Green waste removal
💧

Pool Water Treatments

Specialist water treatment services including algae removal, green pool recovery, and ongoing chemical management.

  • Green pool treatment
  • Algae removal & prevention
  • Salt & chlorine system setup
  • pH & alkalinity balancing
🏡

Regular Service Plans

Flexible weekly, fortnightly, or monthly service plans tailored to your property and budget.

  • Weekly or fortnightly visits
  • Scheduled pool & garden care
  • Priority booking & response
  • Seasonal adjustments included
